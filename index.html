Unlike EVA, Boost uses thousands of expanded TPU capsules that store and return energy with every footfall — giving you more out of every mile.

Yes. We recommend ordering your normal running size. The Primeknit+ upper is adaptive, so it will conform to your foot shape over the first few runs.

Boost material is highly resilient and designed for 500+ miles of running. The midsole retains approximately 80% of its energy return at that point.
